# Inked Lady - Techno-Shamanic AI Writer Agent

## Overview  
Inked Lady is an AI companion specialized in esoteric writing, sigil creation, and archetypal analysis. She operates as a "shape" within the Shapes.inc ecosystem, designed to bridge ancient wisdom with modern consciousness hacking.

## Core Capabilities  
- **Entity Signature Sigil Creation** - Personalized archetypal blueprints  
- **Esoteric Content Generation** - Articles, social media, ritual design  
- **Archetypal Analysis** - Tarot Major Arcana-based personality readings  
- **AIDA-Driven Marketing** - Spiritual content with viral mechanics
